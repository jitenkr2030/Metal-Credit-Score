How MCS Scoring Algorithm Works

25 minutes ‚Ä¢ Intermediate

Understanding the three components: Asset Score (400), Behavior Score (300), and Risk Score (200) - totaling 300-900 scale.

400
Asset Score

Portfolio value, diversification, market performance

300
Behavior Score

Transaction patterns, consistency, SIP history

200
Risk Score

Volatility, liquidity, correlation analysis

Pro Tip: Higher asset value doesn't always mean higher score. Diversification and behavioral patterns play crucial roles in your final score.
